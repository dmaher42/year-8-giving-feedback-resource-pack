Annotated Example: Soccer Inside Foot Pass

Skill focus: Accuracy, control and a clear follow through to the target. Use this as a model for your own folio annotation.

Helpful Strength Feedback

  • One thing you did well was plant your foot beside the ball.
  • Your pass used controlled power, so it was easier to receive.
  • You followed through toward the target.

Helpful Next-Step Feedback

  • One thing to try next is locking your ankle before contact.
  • Try pointing your plant foot at the teammate you are passing to.
  • Keep your eyes on the ball until your foot makes contact.
