Annotated Example: Tennis Forehand

Skill focus: Grip, preparation, contact and follow through. Use this as a model for your own folio annotation.

Helpful Strength Feedback

  • One thing you did well was turning side-on before hitting.
  • Your contact point was in front, which helped the ball travel forward.
  • You finished balanced and ready for the next shot.

Helpful Next-Step Feedback

  • One thing to try next is taking the racquet back earlier.
  • Try a handshake grip so the racquet face stays controlled.
  • Follow through toward your target instead of stopping at the ball.
