Unit Overview and Rationale

Giving Feedback is a Year 8 Health and Physical Education unit about improving performance through feedback, communication and positive team culture. Students focus on one main sport for several lessons, build connected movement skills, then apply those skills in small games, tactical situations and modified activities.

The central idea is that positive communication makes games more enjoyable and more successful. Students learn how encouragement, calm responses to mistakes, respectful feedback and inclusive behaviour help individuals improve and help teams perform better.

Students learn to
practise movement skills, use feedback, manage frustration, support teammates and reflect on performance improvement.
Assessment evidence
Google Slides folio, peer feedback records, short reflections and teacher observation of practical participation.
Aligned focus
Year 8 HPE movement skills, learning through movement, respectful relationships, fair play, inclusion, collaboration and SA Education dispositions.
