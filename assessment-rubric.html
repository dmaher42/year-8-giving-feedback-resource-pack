Movement skill understandingEmerging
Identifies a few movement skills but explanations are limited or unclear.		Describes some key points for movement skills with partial accuracy.Clearly explains key coaching points and movement concepts for different sports.Explains movement skills accurately and connects technique, movement concepts and game performance.
Skill annotation and analysisAnnotations are incomplete or mostly labels without explanation.Includes basic labels and some common mistakes or corrections.Uses clear annotations, key coaching points, common mistakes and useful corrections linked to control or accuracy.Uses detailed, accurate annotations that analyse how technique, timing, force, space or contact point affects performance.
Quality of feedbackFeedback is general, unclear or may not help the performer know what to do next.Feedback includes a strength or next step but needs more detail.Feedback is specific, respectful and includes a useful coaching cue based on observation.Feedback is thoughtful, encouraging and clearly matched to the performer's needs, skill level and emotional response.
Reflection on performance improvementReflection gives limited detail about improvement.Reflection describes some improvement but may not explain what caused it.Reflection explains how feedback and practice helped improve control, accuracy or decision-making.Reflection uses strong examples to evaluate which feedback or strategies had the biggest effect over time.
Teamwork and communicationShows limited evidence of positive communication or teamwork.Usually participates and communicates with some reminders.Communicates respectfully, listens to others and supports team cohesion and performance.Strengthens team cohesion by encouraging others, including peers, solving communication problems and supporting emotional regulation.
Contribution to positive team cultureMay blame, argue, exclude others or need support to respond positively after mistakes.Sometimes encourages others and usually avoids unhelpful behaviours with reminders.Encourages others, responds respectfully, supports peers and helps the team stay positive during challenges.Consistently models positive leadership, helps teammates reset, includes others and improves the enjoyment and success of the group.
Leadership, fair play and inclusivityNeeds support to show fair play, inclusion or emotional regulation.Shows fair play and inclusion in some activities.Usually demonstrates leadership, fair play, inclusion, safety awareness and calm responses during challenges.Consistently models leadership, fair play, empathy, resilience and responsibility, and suggests modifications that help everyone participate.
